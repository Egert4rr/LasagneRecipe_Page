Lasagne

Ingredient Amount
Ground meat 1.5 kg
Lasagne sheets 20 pieces
Tomato sauce 3 jars of 500 g
Grated cheese 500 g
  • Step 1 - Cook the ground meat in a pan
  • Step 2 - Add the tomato sauce to the cooked ground meat, mix and cook
  • Step 3 - Prepare and oven pan with cooking paper
  • Step 4 - Add a layer of 5 lasagne sheets and cover it with the sauce, repeat this until you are out of sauce or sheets
  • Step 5 - Put the oven to cook from both sides and on 180 degrees
  • Step 6 - Put your lasagne into the oven for 30 min
